ABOUT US

Welcome to the **GameDev & Esports Club of IIT Guwahati**! We are a vibrant community dedicated to fostering a passion for both game development and competitive esports.

Our club brings together students from diverse backgrounds, providing a platform to learn, collaborate, and excel in the exciting world of gaming. Whether you're interested in designing and coding your own games, or honing your skills to compete at the highest level in popular esports titles, you'll find your place here.

We organize workshops, hackathons, gaming tournaments, and knowledge-sharing sessions to help members grow. Join us to turn your gaming dreams into reality!
